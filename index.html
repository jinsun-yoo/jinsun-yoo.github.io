Jinsun Yoo

I am a 5th year Ph.D. candidate in Georgia Tech, advised by Profs. Kishore Ramachandran and Tushar Krishna. Prior to coming to Atlanta, I received my Bachelor's degree in Seoul National University (SNU), Korea.

My dissertation research aims to make it easier to predict the performance of distributed ML workloads. The work looks at several layers of the configuration space - workload, collective, network fabric- and combines simulation with ground truth, in stack execution. My CV is available here.

At my free time I play the double bass as a proud member of the Emory University Symphony Orchestra (EUSO).
